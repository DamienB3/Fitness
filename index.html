Get Started

Before working out, ALWAYS remember to:
  • ✅ Practice good form.
  • ✅ Safely warm-up beforehand.
  • ✅ Drink plenty of water.
  • ✅ If you are at a gym, please practice social distancing.
  • ✅ Pace yourself: Fitness is a lifetime commitment.
  • ✅ Listen to your body: Take a rest day if needed, do NOT overexert yourself.
